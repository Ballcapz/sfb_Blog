My Goals

My current goals are to discover whether or not I like to shape and finish surboards and stand-up paddle boards. With that goal in mind, I am working towards learning more and more everyday to hopefully become skilled at shaping and finishing boards.

About Me

My name is Zach Johnson and I am from central Minnesota. I know that shaping surfboards may seem like kind of an odd thing for me to partake in, but I got the idea when I spent the day on a lake paddling around a stand-up paddle board a few weeks ago. I got to thinking that it might be pretty cool to shape a board of my own, so the next weekend I went to town and picked up what I needed to get started and from now on we'll see where it takes me.
